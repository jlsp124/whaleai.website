How it will work

High level workflow

We keep the process simple and transparent. You define the rules. You control the wallet.

Discover wallets

Follow a curated or custom list of public wallets and monitor swaps in real time.

Set rules

Define limits per wallet: min/max size, exposure, TP/SL, and daily caps.

Automate safely

Automations only execute if your safety rules and caps pass.

Review results

Track logs, open positions, and summaries without hiding anything.
