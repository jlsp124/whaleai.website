How it works

  1. 1

    Link your wallet

    Connect with Phantom from the bot. No seed phrases, no private keys.

  2. 2

    Add custom whales

    Follow wallets you trust and configure min/max size, exposure, TP/SL.

  3. 3

    Start in paper mode

    Simulate the pipeline end-to-end and review logs before going live.

  4. 4

    Go live via vault (optional)

    Fund a program-owned vault and trade through the Solana program with an allowance.
