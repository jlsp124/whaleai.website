Privacy Policy

Effective date: update this before launch.

1) Data we collect

  • Telegram username (required)
  • Email (optional)
  • Heard about / referral source (optional)
  • Attribution ref and assigned referral code
  • IP hash and user agent (abuse prevention)

2) Why we collect it

We use the data to manage the waitlist, prevent abuse, and measure referral attribution. We do not sell your data.

3) IP hashing

We never store raw IP addresses. We store a one-way hash of IP + a secret salt for abuse prevention.

4) Data retention

We retain waitlist data until it is no longer needed for beta operations. You can request deletion by contacting support.

5) Third-party processors

We use Cloudflare (Workers, D1, KV) to process and store waitlist data.

6) Contact

For privacy requests, contact support.
