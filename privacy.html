Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-15

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what Whale AI collects and how it is used. Whale AI is designed to be non-custodial: we do not request or store seed phrases or private keys.

2. What we store

CategoryExamples
Account identifiersTelegram ID (tg_id)
Wallet metadataPublic wallet address, vault address (PDA), allowance settings
Trading preferencesFollowed whales, TP/SL, min/max sizes, exposure caps, notifications
Trade recordsPaper trade logs, positions, realized PnL, timestamps, simulated/real tx ids
PaymentsPayment intent metadata, pack type, amount, verified transaction signature

3. What we do not collect

  • Seed phrases or private keys
  • Phantom secret keys

4. How we use data

  • Provide the bot features (wallet linking, rules, copy-trading, logs)
  • Verify on-chain payments for credits/Pro
  • Operate an admin dashboard for monitoring and support
  • Debug and improve reliability

5. Sharing

Whale AI may query third-party services (e.g., Solana RPC providers, Jupiter price APIs) to power features. Whale AI does not sell personal data.

6. Data retention

We keep data only as long as needed for operating the service. You can request deletion by contacting support.
