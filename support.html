What is Whale AI?

Whale AI is a non-custodial automation tool that follows public wallet activity using configurable rules.

Is Whale AI custodial?

No. Whale AI never requests seed phrases or private keys.

Is this financial advice?

No. Whale AI is tooling only and provides no investment advice.
