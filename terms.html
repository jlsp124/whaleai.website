1. Important notices

  • Not financial advice. Whale AI is an automation tool; crypto trading is risky and can result in total loss.
  • Non-custodial. Whale AI never asks for seed phrases or private keys, and does not custody user funds.
  • Paper mode vs live mode. Paper mode simulates trades; live mode can execute real on-chain transactions and may lose funds.

2. The service

Whale AI is a Telegram bot and web interface that can link a wallet, let you follow whale wallets, and execute or simulate copy-trades according to your rules.

3. Wallet linking and vault allowances

When you link a wallet, you may also initialize an on-chain vault (a program-derived address controlled by the Whale AI Solana program) and set an allowance. You control whether trading is active and can withdraw at any time, subject to on-chain rules and network conditions.

4. Fees, credits and Pro

  • Credits: 1 credit per executed trade (buy or sell) in the bot.
  • Pro: monthly plan with unlimited trades during the active period.
  • No performance fees: Whale AI does not charge a percentage of profits.
  • On-chain payments: transactions are final once confirmed.

5. Your responsibilities

  • Keep control of your wallet and review every on-chain approval and transaction.
  • Use sensible risk limits and only trade what you can afford to lose.
  • Comply with laws and regulations in your jurisdiction.

6. Limitations and third parties

Whale AI depends on third-party networks and services (e.g., Solana RPC providers, Jupiter pricing APIs). Outages, congestion, inaccurate pricing, and other failures may occur.
